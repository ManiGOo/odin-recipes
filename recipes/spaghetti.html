Million Dollar Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
